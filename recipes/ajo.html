Ajo Campero

Ingredients

  • 1 kg stale crusty bread
  • 1 kg tomatoes
  • 1/2 head of garlic
  • 2 bell peppers
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • salt
  • 2 soft boiled eggs
  • 1-2 pickled bell peppers, sliced

Steps

  1. Tear the bread into coarse pieces and set aside
  2. Char the bell peppers and slice into pieces; set aside
  3. Boil the eggs and set aside
  4. Blanch tomatoes 30-60 sec until cracks form, peel once cool enough, and set aside both tomatoes and cooking liquid, keeping the liquid warm
  5. In a large pot or basin, crush garlic and salt until a coarse paste forms
  6. Repeat with bell peppers and peeled tomatoes (one at a time)
  7. As the mixture becomes liquidy, add pieces of bread to absorb liquid
  8. Add the rest of the bread and continue mixing
  9. Add a generous glug of olive oil
  10. Add tomato cooking liquid as required to moisten the mixture, mix, and cover
  11. Let rest for 5-10 minutes, uncover, and mix
  12. Serve immediately topped with quartered eggs and pickled pepper
