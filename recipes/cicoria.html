Chicory and Beans

Ingredients

  • 2-3 heads chicory
  • 16 oz can cannelini or borlotti beans or substitute
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • 1 chili finely chopped, or pinch pepper flakes
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Wash chicory thoroughly, remove roots, and chop coarsely
  2. Boil in salted water 5-10 minutes until tender, reserve water
  3. Lightly brown garlic in skillet in generous quanity olive oil, add chili at end of cooking
  4. Remove garlic and chili and simmer 5 minutes
  5. Add beans, some bean liquid, some cooking water, and cook for roughly 5 minutes until liquid is thickening and chicory is very tender
  6. Season to taste and serve with starch of your choice, topped with olive oil
